Good to Know

Frequently
asked questions

How do I book you?

Start with the inquiry form. Once we've confirmed your date and details, a 25% non-refundable deposit and a signed contract secure your spot. The remaining balance is due one week before your wedding.

Do you travel to me?

Yes! I'm based in Denver and serving Colorado, Florida & beyond. On-location travel is billed at $35/hour so I can come to your venue or getting-ready suite.

Do you offer trials?

I do — trials are $175 and highly recommended. It's the best way to perfect your look and walk into your wedding day feeling completely confident.

How far in advance should I book?

As early as you can. Peak wedding dates fill quickly, so reaching out early gives you the best chance of securing your date.

Can you style my whole bridal party?

Absolutely. Bridal party styling is $160 per person and flower girls (under 10) are $55. Include your party size in your inquiry and I'll put together a custom quote and timeline.

Do you do extensions?

Yes — I can install client-provided extensions for added length and volume for an additional $45.

What is the early start fee?

For getting-ready times that begin before 7am, a $125 early start fee applies.

How does payment work?

Online payments are preferred. Payment is made as a single balance for the booking rather than split individually between people.
