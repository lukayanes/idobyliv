Meet the Stylist

Hi, I'm Liv

It all began with braiding hair in a Kansas bedroom. Since then, hairstyling has held a special place in my heart — and today, I Do By Liv is a premier beauty practice offering effortless, intentional styling for your wedding day.

Based in Denver, CO, I bring a serene, grounded energy to your morning so you can look and feel exactly how you want — radiant, confident, and completely present for your celebration. My work is all about enhancing your natural beauty with looks that are intentional, timeless, and genuinely you — whether that's intricate braids, sleek styling, soft waves, or an elegant updo.

I Do By Liv operates in both Colorado and Florida, and I'm always happy to travel wherever your love story unfolds.
