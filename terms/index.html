Services

We provide on-location bridal and special-event hairstyling in Colorado, Florida, and beyond. Final pricing depends on the services selected, party size, location, and timing, and will be confirmed before booking.

Booking & Payment

  • A 25% non-refundable deposit and a signed contract are required to reserve your date.
  • The remaining balance is due one week before your wedding or event.
  • Online payment is preferred, and payment is made as a single balance for the booking rather than split individually.
  • On-location travel is billed at $35/hour. Add-ons (extensions, blow-dry, early-start fee) are quoted in advance.

Cancellations & Rescheduling

The deposit is non-refundable. If you need to reschedule, we'll do our best to accommodate your new date subject to availability. Cancellations do not entitle you to a refund of the deposit.

Client Responsibilities

  • Communicate any allergies, sensitivities, or special requests in advance.
