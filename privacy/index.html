Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

I Do By Liv ("we," "us," or "Liv") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or reach out to us, and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry through our contact form, we collect the details you choose to provide, such as your:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Wedding or event date, venue or location
  • Service interest, number of people needing hair, and any message you send

We may also collect limited, non-identifying technical information (such as general site-usage data) to help the website function and improve.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and communicate with you
  • To provide quotes, schedule trials, and coordinate wedding-day services
  • To manage bookings, contracts, and payments

How We Store & Share It

Inquiry details are stored using trusted third-party tools we use to run the business (for example, Google for forms, spreadsheets, and email). We do not sell your personal information, and we only share it with service providers as needed to operate our business.

Cookies & Third Parties

This site uses minimal cookies and may load third-party resources such as web fonts. Any third-party services have their own privacy practices.

Your Choices

You may request to access, update, or delete the information you've shared with us at any time by emailing idobyliv@idobyliv.com.

Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email idobyliv@idobyliv.com.
